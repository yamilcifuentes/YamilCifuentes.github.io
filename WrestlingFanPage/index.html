What are we about!

All Wrestling Pro is a dedicated wrestling fan page where we bring wrestling content across promotions such as the World Champions, Video Games, Shows, etc. Please explore the greatness!

Champions

All Wrestling Pro will provide all of the wrestlers who won the World Heavyweight Champion of the promotion. We will not be including champions such as United States Champion, Untercontinental Championship and Television Championship. We will not show repeated champions

Video Games

All Wrestling Pro will provide the video games that were created for WWE, TNA Wrestling, WCW, etc. There are also unbranded video games such as the recently release Fire Pro Wrestling World.
